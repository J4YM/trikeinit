Introduction

Tricycles are a common mode of transportation in small towns such as Baliwag. However, the lack of a centralized and modernized system often leads to inefficiencies, and inconvenient, experience for both riders and passengers. By this, the researchers have developed an app that aims to address these issues by introducing a comprehensive mobile application that facilitates seamless tricycle transportation.

TRIKE is a web based application that lets the user easily locate a nearby rider for seemingless experience when in need of this specific mode of transportation. It also gives the riders a chance to easily pick up passengers even on a normal day, instead of lining up through a system called “TODA”. It enhances the user experience and gives a lot of opportunities to riders for their own convenience that would mean a Win-Win situation for both sides.

Significance of the Project

The development and implementation of TRIKE would mean a significant change to the digitalization of the Tricycle Transportation community that could pose a boost in economic growth, quantity of users, and overall quality of service. It would also carry evident significance, addressing several aspects of tricycle transportation in urban and suburban areas.

  1. Enhanced Accessibility: TRIKE ensures increased accessibility to reliable and efficient tricycle services, particularly benefiting individuals who heavily rely on this mode of transportation for daily commuting. It also focuses on foreigners and other individuals that are new to the area where they can easily book a ride to anywhere in the city.
  2. Urban Mobility Improvement: By optimizing tricycle services through a centralized app, TRIKE contributes to the overall improvement of urban mobility. It helps lessen traffic congestion and enhances the efficiency of transportation networks in less populated areas.
  3. Safety Enhancement: The incorporation of safety features, including driver verification and emergency assistance, enhances the overall safety of tricycle transportation. TRIKE prioritizes the well-being of both riders and passengers, and at the same time, brings confidence with a sense of safety to users.
  4. Digital Inclusion: TRIKE introduces digital inclusion to a traditionally offline and informal sector. By bringing tricycle services onto a digital platform, the project ensures that even individuals without knowledge about the local transportation within the can participate in
  5. Community Engagement: TRIKE promotes community engagement by establishing partnerships with local tricycle associations such as “TODA” and businesses. This collaboration not only strengthens the tricycle market but also promotes a sense of community and shared responsibility.
  6. Technological Advancement in Transportation: The introduction of TRIKE represents a technological leap in the transportation sector. It showcases the potential for technology to transform traditional modes of transport, for innovation and adaptation in the local transportation industry.

Key Features

  1. User-Friendly Interface:
    - Intuitive and easy-to-navigate app design for both riders and operators.
    - Registration and profile creation for personalized user experiences.
  2. Booking and Scheduling:
    - Real-time tricycle availability status.
    - Advance booking and scheduling options for user convenience.
  3. GPS Integration:
    - Accurate real-time tracking of tricycle location.
    - Estimated time of arrival (ETA) for improved trip planning.
  4. Fare Transparency:
    - Clear and transparent pricing structures.
    - In-app fare estimate based on distance and time.
  5. . Payment Options and Plans:
    - Secure in-app payment options, including cashless transactions.
    - Affordable Rider and Passenger Plans
  6. Safety Features:
    - Driver verification and background checks.
    - Emergency assistance and SOS button for quick response.

Target Market

The primary target market includes residents and commuters in urban and suburban areas where tricycles are a common mode of transportation. TRIKE will focus on regions with high tricycle density and a growing demand for efficient transportation solutions.

The market also encompasses individuals who lack the knowledge about the area where the digitized transportation implementation takes place such as Foreigners, and People who are new to the area. This is a leverage towards the overall community of local transportation within the region.
